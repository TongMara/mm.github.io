Introducation about Her

A cute but somewhat bossy little pig, four years old. Lives with Mummy Pig, Daddy Pig and her brother George.

Peppa enjoys hanging out with her best friend Suzy, visiting Grandma and Grandpa Pig, and taking care of George. On top of that, Paige loves bouncing around in muddy puddles, laughing and snoring loudly.

Her Hobbies

Peppa Pig's favorite things to do are play games, dress up, go on vacation, and jump up and down happily in the little mud pit.

In addition to these, she also likes to explore everywhere. Although she sometimes encounters some small situations, she can always save the day, and it will bring everyone unexpected surprises.
